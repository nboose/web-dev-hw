An Overview of the Book:

With crisp nature photography and clear, concise words, The Elusive Here & Now
offers inspiring perspectives on life, reality, and the choices we make.

Written in a humble, yet nearly hypnotic style, the poetry gently opens eyes
and hearts with ideas about who we are, how we create, and what we know to be true in the moment.

Carole Hodges

Each poem is a refreshing surprise that awakens us to our humanity.

Denis Waitley

A poet more like a prophet, with a message that stirs our values at the core, Dan Coppersmith pierces our skin-deep culture, going straight to the heart and soul with words that inspire us to greatness.
