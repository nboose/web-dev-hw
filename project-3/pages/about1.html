About the
Author. . .

Dan Coppersmith

I am Dan Coppersmith, the poet, photographer, healer and teacher behind the inspirational works presented here on this site.

I started this journey of self-esteem advocacy when I woke up one day and found that I was living a life of who I thought I was supposed to be (good provider, hard worker, etc), but I had lost the inner peace of full self-expression. I was missing the joy that comes from just being who you are.

I embarked on an adventure of self-discovery and personal improvement that allowed me to let go of many limiting beliefs and opened me to new possibilities. (. . . Imagine a 19-year veteran of Test Engineering in the very logic-oriented semiconductor industry suddenly leaving my corporate job and taking art classes and writing poetry!)

After 40 plus years of doing what I was told, I was finally discovering who I am and the brilliance inside of me. I like what I found!

My Mission in Life Is to:

Enjoy each moment and help others experience the joy of living and celebrating who they are.
