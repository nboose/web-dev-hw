ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Natalie Elizabeth Boose, and I am currently a student at the University of Montana.
Right now, I am in my senior year studying for a degree in management & entrepreneurship. My hope, in the future, is to
start-up several miscellaneous businesses. So far, so good! If you are in the Missoula area, check out my very first LLC,
GOD SHOT ESPRESSO BAR! Drop my name, and you'll get a great deal. :)

However, in my spare time, apart from running my very own business and finishing my education, I like
to take short breaks to do creative-type things. After all, for an individual to function at optimal levels, it is important
to switch from left brain to right brain on a regular basis. Hence, my go-to happy place is Zentangle. Because of this, I
I thought I would share a short tangle with my friends here online, to see if it would be something they would enjoy as well.

Drawing a zentangle only takes a couple minutes, and can be exactly the thing you need to give your tired
mind a break from the tasks of daily life. Please enjoy the short instructional that I have posted, and make sure to post
your own creations on the separate page I have provided for you. Thanks!
